System Intel FAQ

01 // Breeding Power Calculation expand_more
Our engine uses the internal breeding power values for every Pal, calculating the average and rounding to the nearest index result. We track the most recent values from version 1.x data dumps.
02 // Update Protocol Frequency expand_more
Data updates are pushed within 24 hours of any official patch or major expansion. The Feybreak expansion data was integrated in real-time during the launch window.
03 // Legendary Breeding Constraints expand_more
Legendary Pals typically require specific parent pairs or same-species breeding. Our calculator explicitly highlights these "Special Combinations" to avoid failed breeding attempts.
04 // Passive Inheritance Logic expand_more
While the child species is fixed, passive traits follow probability weights. Our tactical guide (available via the Passives link) details the exact odds of 1, 2, 3, or 4 trait transfers.
05 // Gender Impact Assessment expand_more
Gender does not affect the resulting species, but it is the primary bottleneck for breeding loops. Our reverse lookup tool can help you identify alternative paths if you're stuck with only one gender.
06 // Offline Access Protocol expand_more
PalBreed is built as a PWA (Progressive Web App). Once loaded, the breeding engine remains functional even without a persistent data connection.
