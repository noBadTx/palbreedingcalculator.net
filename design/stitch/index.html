Mission Intel (FAQ)

Technical data regarding breeding mechanics.

How does Pal breeding power work?

Every Pal has a hidden breeding power value. The child is determined by the average power of the two parents, mapping to the Pal with the closest power level.

Is the data updated for v1.x?

Yes, our dataset includes all Pals from the latest Feybreak and Sakurajima updates, including the new subspecies and raid bosses.

Can I breed legendary Pals?

Legendary Pals like Frostallion or Jetragon usually require two parents of the same species to breed. Our calculator highlights these special cases.

How are passive skills inherited?

Passives have a probability-based inheritance system. While the species is guaranteed, the skills are a mix of parent traits and random mutations.

Does the gender of the parents matter?

For the resulting species, gender does not matter as long as you have one male and one female. Only power values are calculated.

Is there an offline version?

This site is a Progressive Web App (PWA). Once loaded, the core calculator logic works entirely in your browser without needing a stable connection.
