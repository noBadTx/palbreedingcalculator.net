What Are Cookies

Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit a website. They help us understand how visitors use our Site and improve functionality.

How We Use Cookies

We use cookies for the following purposes:

  • **Essential cookies**: Required for the Site to function. These cannot be disabled.
  • **Analytics cookies**: Used by Plausible Analytics and Google Analytics 4 to understand how visitors interact with the Site. These are only set with your consent.

Third-Party Cookies

The following third-party services may set cookies on our Site:

  • **Google Analytics 4**: sets cookies to track visits and interactions. You can learn more at [Google's Privacy Policy](https://policies.google.com/privacy).
  • **Plausible Analytics**: does not use cookies by default and collects minimal anonymous data.

Managing Your Preferences

When you first visit the Site, you can choose which cookies you accept. You can change your preferences at any time by clicking the cookie settings link in the footer.

You can also disable cookies through your browser settings. Please note that some features may not work properly if essential cookies are disabled.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Cookie Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated date.
