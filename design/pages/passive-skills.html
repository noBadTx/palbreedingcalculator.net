Palworld Passive Skills Guide

Passive skills can turn a good Pal into a great one. Learn which passives stack, which are best for combat or work, and how to plan your breeding around them.

Combat Passives

  • Legend +15% ATK
  • Serious +10% ATK
  • Brave +5% ATK
  • Lucky +5% Crit

Work Passives

  • Artisan +50% Work Speed
  • Workaholic +30% Work Speed
  • Diet Lover +10% Hunger Resist
  • Hard Worker +20% Work Speed

How Passives Inherit

When two Pals breed, the child can inherit passives from either parent. The more parents share the same passive, the higher the chance it transfers. Plan your breeding chain to stack the best passives onto your final target Pal.
